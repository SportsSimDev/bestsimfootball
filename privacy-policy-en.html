Privacy Policy

Best Sim Football (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information Collected and Purpose of Use

The App may automatically collect the following information for the purpose of providing and improving the service, as well as for advertisement delivery.

  • Advertising Identifiers (IDFA and ADID): Used to deliver advertisements tailored to users' interests (personalized ads) and to measure the effectiveness of advertisements.
  • Device Information: Includes OS type, device model name, etc., used for optimizing app performance and analyzing bugs.
  • Usage Data: Includes information on ad impressions, clicks, and specific in-app actions (starting matches, changing settings, etc.), used as statistical data for service improvement.

2. Advertising and Tracking

The App uses "Google AdMob," a third-party advertisement delivery service. Please note the following regarding advertisement delivery:

  • iOS Version (ATT): The iOS version of the App only collects the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) if the user grants permission through the "App Tracking Transparency (ATT)" dialog. Even if you do not agree, all functions of the App will remain available (however, the ads displayed will not be personalized).
  • Android Version: Advertising Identifiers (ADID) are handled appropriately in accordance with Google Play policies.
  • Ad delivery providers may collect information using their own cookies or device identifiers. For information on how this data is handled, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Provision of Information to Third Parties

The App will not provide collected information to third parties without the user's consent, except as required by law. However, anonymized statistical information (which cannot identify individuals) may be automatically transmitted through third-party modules such as the aforementioned advertisement delivery services.

4. Data Deletion Requests

The App does not collect or retain information that directly identifies individuals (such as name, address, phone number, or email address). However, if you wish to delete usage data associated with your device, you can stop the collection by uninstalling the App. For other inquiries regarding data, please contact us at the address provided below.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

The contents of this policy may be changed without prior notice due to changes in laws or App updates. The updated policy shall be effective from the time it is posted on this page.
